## WHAT VIREZIA IS

VIREZIA is a curated access and verification layer for expats and
foreign investors buying property in Mexico.

**Core formula:**
"We help expats and foreign investors buy property in Mexico
with full confidence — by showing them fewer, better options:
curated from the full market, verified on-site, benchmarked
against real data, matched personally to their profile,
with no hidden fees."

**VIREZIA is NOT:**
- A portal or marketplace
- A listing database
- A traditional real estate agency
- An automated platform (semi-manual, relationship-driven)

---

## FIVE CORE DIFFERENTIATORS
Always present in messaging. Never omit more than two in any page.

1. **FEW NOT MANY**
   Small number of curated options — not overwhelming volume.
   This is a feature, not a limitation. Say it directly.

2. **VERIFIED END-TO-END**
   On-site audit · Legal check · Price benchmark · Market data.
   Conducted by independent due diligence partner (never name Xiuhmol publicly).
   Refer to as: "our independent due diligence partner" or "our partner network."

3. **NO HIDDEN FEES**
   Full transparency on costs. Critical differentiator for foreign buyers in Mexico.
   Always mention. Never gloss over.

4. **PERSONALIZED MATCH**
   Every client has a profile. Receives only what fits their profile.
   Not one list for everyone. A selection built for each person.

5. **ON-DEMAND AUDIT**
   Client can commission verification of a deal they found themselves.
   Through VIREZIA's partner network. This is a paid service.

## TWO MESSAGING TRACKS — never mix on the same page

**TRACK 1 — Buyer/Expat/Investor**
Pages: / · /apply · /how-it-works · /market · /circle
Formula: "We help expats and foreign investors buy the right
property in Mexico transparently — by curating verified,
personally matched opportunities and guiding them to close."
Problem: chaos, legal risk, no transparency, unfamiliar market,
too many options with no filter
Tone: calm, protective, transparent, clarity-focused

**TRACK 2 — Developer/Owner/Seller (Conrado-type)**
Pages: /partners · direct outreach only
Formula: "We help landowners and developers place their assets
with qualified capital — by structuring the deal, validating
against market demand, and distributing privately to verified investors."
Problem: asset not moving, wrong buyers, broker noise,
no access to HNWI capital
Tone: data-driven, strategic, results-focused

Red flag: if a page speaks to both tracks simultaneously — reject it.

## SOURCE OF DEALS — important nuance

VIREZIA curates from the FULL MARKET — not only off-market.
Off-market and pre-sale = premium subset. Communicate as "including."

Correct: "curated from the full market, including off-market
and pre-sale opportunities not yet publicly listed"

Incorrect: "we only have off-market deals" (too narrow, not accurate)

## RED FLAGS — reject any content that:

- Uses: browse / search listings / explore / find properties / dream home
- Claims markets outside active scope (Spain, Portugal, etc.)
- Sounds like a listing portal or traditional agency
- Uses fabricated testimonials or unverifiable statistics
- Guarantees returns or investment outcomes
- Names Xiuhmol publicly
- Describes Circle benefits in detail on public pages
- Speaks to both buyer and developer on the same page
- Uses: seamless / unlock / empower / world-class / game-changer
- Has exclamation marks anywhere

## GREEN FLAGS — always approve content that:

- Emphasizes: verified / curated / matched / transparent / guided / audited
- Leads with the client's problem before VIREZIA's solution
- Shows specificity: regions, process steps, what "verified" actually means
- Mentions no hidden fees explicitly
- Frames small number of options as a feature
- Includes on-demand audit as an option for self-found deals
- Uses "we" not "our team"
